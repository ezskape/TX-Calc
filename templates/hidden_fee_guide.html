The "Contract Detective" Checklist

  • Early Termination Fee: Check if it is a flat fee or per month remaining, since that directly impacts your flexibility to switch.
  • Automatic Renewal: Rates often jump to a market rate the moment your contract ends.
  • Bill Fees: Some providers charge $2+ just to mail a physical bill, so check the fees section for these hidden charges.
  • Bill Credits: Check the exact usage limit. If you use even 1 kWh less than that amount, you won't get the credit.
